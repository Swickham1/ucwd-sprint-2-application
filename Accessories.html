Guitar picks have varying pick sizes from 0.3mm - 8.0mm

Amps

Amps are essential if you plan on picking up an electric guitar
The kind of amp you purchase really depends on the amount of money you are willing to spend

Guitar Strings

Guitar Strings are important, and required to make a guitar function
There are many types of guitar strings that you can choose from, some strings are made for specific kinds of guitars e.g nylon strings are meant for classical guitars while steel strings are meant for electric
guitar strings also have different gauges or thicknesses, the gauge of a string changes the sound of the string, its down to preference on what gauge you want to play on

Pedals

Pedals are option but recommended for electric guitars as they enhance the sound of your guitars through your amp
There are two types of pedals: Digital and analog
Digital pedals are good for beginners as they are able to produce many effects in one pedal, the only downside is that they might not sound as good as analog
Analog pedals produce one effect at a higher sound quality output and are better for those who know what kind of sound theyre looking for, it is also possible to chain analog pedals together to combine multiple effects to your guitar.
